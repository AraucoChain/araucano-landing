Araucano

The Open Finance Layer.

Araucano is a decentralized Open Finance ecosystem, composed of a Blockchain network focused on the financial industry (Arauco Chain), a neobank for business and B2B transactions (Arauco Bank), and a network of financial applications connected through a single interface in the neobank (BankApps).

About

Araucano is the layer for Decentralized Open Finance, to enable Fintechs with Blockchain apps, to bring Web3 to the next billion people and companies.

Integrated

Thousands of financial applications and networks in a single user interface, with Arauco Bank you will have a neobank and a complete ecosystem at your fingertips.

Cross Platform

Use Araucano products from any device, without having to manage complex wallets or understand in detail how Blockchain works.

Fast

Thousands of transactions per second at very low cost, so that your only concern is your work and not sending money, especially for international payments.

Learn More

A complete ecosystem (Fintechs and Web3 apps) within your reach in a simple way.

Features

Here are some features for our open, multi-protocol and standards-compliant financial network.

Fast Processing

Thousands of transactions per second on Arauco Chain, our high-performance network, optimized for the financial industry

EVM-compatible

Arauco Chain is an EVM-compatible network, so you can migrate your smart contracts with very little development and cost.

Interoperability

Connect, send and receive money from other Blockchain networks and financial networks, so you won't need to migrate your assets from one place to another

Compliance

Arauco Chain and Arauco Bank will be compatible with various financial and international regulations to offer a reliable experience for users and partners

Save Money

Send any amount and type of money/asset for just a few cents to anywhere in the world

BankApps

Arauco Bank will allow Fintechs and Web3 apps created in Arauco Chain to connect, so that they can reach millions of users in a single interface. Arauco Bank will be a "SuperApp"

Frequently Asked Questions

Here we answer some frequently asked questions. If you have any additional questions, please feel free to contact us using the form below.

What is Arauco Chain?

Arauco Chain is a Blockchain network optimized for financial applications, allowing Financial institutions, Fintechs, and Web3 developers to create enterprise-grade services for their clients.
Arauco Chain website

What is Arauco Bank?

Arauco Bank is a neobank built on Arauco Chain, focused on local and international B2B transactions, with the experience of a bank and the advantages of a Blockchain network.
Arauco Bank documentation

Opportunities with Arauco Chain

Create financial applications with Fintech modules that we are integrating natively into the network, allowing you to accelerate development and focus on your business rather than development. Additionally, you will be able to use stablecoins, tokenized deposits, and much more.

BankApps

Arauco Bank will not only be a neobank, but also a "SuperApp", where other applications (Fintechs and Web3) can be integrated in plug and play mode (BankApps) to offer their services to users in a single interface, accessing more opportunities for both platforms and users.

How can I create applications on the network?

Our network is EVM compatible, so you can see the details and build your applications and smart contracts on the first devnet in any compatible framework. Check out the documentation for links and details of the network.
